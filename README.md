# ZeroKelvinNoise

An exploration of Zero-Noise Extrapolation (ZNE) methods for quantum computing, inspired by a [past submission](https://github.com/RasmitDevkota/QOSF2024/fork) to the Quantum Open Source Foundation's 2024 mentorship application.

I explore three primary modifications to the typical ZNE methodology:

1. Bayesian curve fitting (bayes.ipynb)
2. Kolomogorov-Smirov test-based compound Gaussian-Poisson distribution fitting (ks.ipynb)
3. Maximum likelihood estimation (mle.ipynb)

More approaches to come in the future as I come up with ideas!
